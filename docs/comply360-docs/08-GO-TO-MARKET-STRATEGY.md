## 1. Market Entry Strategy

### 1.1 Launch Approach

**Phase 1: Private Beta** (Month 1-2)
- 10 hand-picked agent partners
- Free access in exchange for feedback
- Weekly check-ins and iterations
- Goal: Validate product-market fit

**Phase 2: Public Beta** (Month 3-4)
- 50 agent partners
- Discounted pricing (50% off first 3 months)
- Self-service onboarding
- Goal: Refine onboarding and scale systems

**Phase 3: General Availability** (Month 5+)
- Full public launch
- Standard pricing
- Marketing campaign activation
- Goal: Rapid customer acquisition

### 1.2 Target Markets

**Primary**: South Africa (70% focus)
- 15,000+ corporate service providers
- 200,000+ annual company registrations
- Existing pain points with CIPC process

**Secondary**: Zimbabwe (30% focus)
- 3,000+ corporate service providers
- 12,000+ annual registrations
- Manual, paper-based processes

**Future**: Botswana, Namibia, Zambia, Mozambique

## 3. Pricing Strategy

### 3.1 Pricing Model

**Tiered Subscription** (SaaS) + **Transaction Commissions**

**Tier 1: Starter** - $199/month
- 1 user
- 20 registrations/month
- Basic features
- Email support
- Target: Solo practitioners

**Tier 2: Professional** - $499/month
- 5 users
- 100 registrations/month
- Advanced features (bulk upload, API access)
- Priority support
- Target: Small firms

**Tier 3: Enterprise** - $999/month
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited registrations
- Custom integrations, white-label branding
- Dedicated account manager
- Target: Large firms

**Add-ons**:
- Extra users: $50/month each
- Extra registrations: $5 each
- Premium support: $200/month
- Custom features: Quoted

**Transaction Commission**: 10-15% of service fees (not government fees)

### 3.2 Pricing Rationale

**Value-Based Pricing**:
- Manual process: $200/registration Ã— 50 registrations = $10,000/month cost
- Comply360: $499/month + $250 commissions = $749/month
- **Savings: $9,251/month (92%)**

**Competitive Pricing**:
- Positioned 20% below traditional service providers
- Premium to basic software (justified by AI features)

## 5. Sales Strategy

### 5.1 Sales Process

**1. Lead Generation**
- Marketing campaigns
- Inbound inquiries
- Outbound prospecting

**2. Qualification** (BANT)
- Budget: Can afford $200-$1,000/month?
- Authority: Decision-maker involved?
- Need: Pain point with current process?
- Timeline: Ready to implement within 3 months?

**3. Demo** (30 minutes)
- Personalized to customer use case
- Show key features (name search, registration wizard, dashboard)
- Emphasize time/cost savings
- Address objections

**4. Trial** (14 days)
- Hands-on access to platform
- Onboarding call with CSM
- Process 1-2 test registrations
- Check-in at day 7

**5. Close**
- Proposal with pricing
- Address final concerns
- Contract signing (DocuSign)
- Onboarding kickoff

**6. Onboarding** (Week 1)
- Setup: Branding, user accounts, integrations
- Training: Video tutorials, live sessions
- First registration: Guided walkthrough
- Success metrics: Complete 5 registrations in first month

## 6. Customer Success

### 6.1 Onboarding Process

**Week 1: Setup**
- Welcome email with getting started guide
- Schedule onboarding call (30 minutes)
- Configure branding and settings
- Add team members

**Week 2: Training**
- Video tutorials (self-paced)
- Live training session (1 hour)
- Practice registrations in sandbox
- Q&A session

**Week 3: First Registration**
- Guided walkthrough of first live registration
- Support team available for questions
- Feedback survey

**Week 4: Check-In**
- CSM check-in call
- Review usage and identify blockers
- Upsell opportunities (additional users, features)

### 6.2 Customer Success Metrics

**Health Score** (0-100):
- Usage: 40 points (logins, registrations processed)
- Engagement: 30 points (feature adoption, support tickets)
- Satisfaction: 30 points (NPS, survey responses)

**Intervention Triggers**:
- Health score < 50: CSM outreach within 24 hours
- No logins for 7 days: Automated email + CSM call
- Multiple support tickets: Escalate to product team

**Churn Prevention**:
- At-risk customers: Monthly check-ins
- Exit interviews: Understand reasons for churn
- Win-back campaigns: Re-engage churned customers after 3 months
