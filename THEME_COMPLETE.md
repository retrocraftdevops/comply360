### **Adding Animations:**

```svelte
<!-- Fade in -->
<div class="animate-fade-in">
  Content
</div>

<!-- Staggered animation -->
{#each items as item, index}
  <div class="animate-fade-in" style="animation-delay: {index * 50}ms">
    {item}
  </div>
{/each}
```
